Price of a call

equity needed = call / final pot

Final pot includes your own call.

Half pot → 20% · two thirds → 25%

Pot → 33% · twice pot → 40%

Outs to equity

x4 with two cards to come, x2 with one

Fold equity

folds needed = bet / (pot + bet)

A bluff needs the same percentage as the price it lays.

Half pot → 33% · pot → 50%

Equity when called lowers the bar.

Implied odds

extra = (1 - e) · call / e - pot

What you must extract later to justify a call that is priced wrong now.

Only count what villain will actually pay, not their stack.

Reading the bar

Green is equity you hold.

Gold is a threshold you must clear.

The white tick is the break-even line. Past it, call. Short of it, fold.
